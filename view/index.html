About the person
 
 

Namaskar, I Praveen Pandey am a small businessman who thinks about his nation & society especially for those poor children who can not afford basic necessities of life

I was a manager for few years in a private firm. When I used to travel and watch those children bare footed on footpath suddenly from inside an urge to help them aroused in my heart. From there my life changed.

I started Kislay seva sansthan in year 2016 and running till date. I hope other people will also rise & stand with us to help these children
